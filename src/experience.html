Programs I Was Apart Of

I was a part of two Apple programs regarding tech in the past year. The first one was GDYT's Code To Career, which was an association with the boys and girls club in my city. In this program we were tasked with created an application that serve the city of Detroit in some way. We were put in teams of 4 and were given resources on how to make an app. The main focus of the program was more on the theoretical side of application development (User Stories, deployment etc).My group created an idea called Talent Talk, which was a way for small, unrecognized talent in Detroit to get noticed. Essentially,the idea was that people can congregate in rooms that were either private or public. In each of these rooms, chosen people would get to perform for a set amount of time, while an audience watches. Think of it as an online version of "America's Got Talent". At the end of the program, each team would present their ideas on stage to an audience of people and my team's idea won. Below is a PDF to the presentation that we won with. The PowerPoint is interactable.

The other program was similar to the GDYT one, however, this was more directly connected to Apple. It was Apple Developer Academy and in this program we were taught how to make more elaborate apps. This program focused more on coding in Swift than the theoretical side of things. Similairly, we were tasked with created an application that served the community and me and my team came up with a program called Fresh. This was an app idea that connects people to their community garden for the purpose of delivering fresh foods to underrepresented groups in Detroit. Below is the PowerPoint presentation
